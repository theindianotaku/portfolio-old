Description

Channel i is the intranet e-portal of IIT Roorkee which provides various services for the students, faculty and alumni of the institute. Noticeboard is a web platform which records the notices by various campus groups, departments and institute authorities. This being accessible only through intranet and hence had the accessibility problem for the users who are outside the campus. To make the platform accessible on-the-go and on the internet, we decided to develop an android app.

This was my first project as a member of IMG. The android app was aimed to provide the basic features of viewing the notices which were basically PDFs. The notices are categorized under Departments and Institute Authorities. The navigation drawer is the primary navigation medium. The app was designed to be a Minimum Value Product due to the restriction on the time to be spent on its development. The next revision of the app was planned out in the sext semester with the inclusion of various primary user based features like making bookmarks, push notification customization, create events and set reminders. The re-design of this app is discussed in the next article.
