Description

Unnat Bharat Abhiyan is inspired by the vision of transformational change in rural development processes by leveraging knowledge institutions to help build the architecture of an Inclusive India.

As a part of the initiative taken up by our Institute, a platform to record and showcase the case studies of various nearby villages was needed. The most suitable platform decided was Web.

I was responsible for the development of the website. Their requirements being; use any free-to-use HTML/CSS template of your choice which is most suitable for this task and to complete this task within a period of 3 days.

My best trusted website for free-to-use responsive-and-customisable web templates is html5up.net. The best suitable template from their catalogue to me was Editorial. This template was the most suitable for content heavy uses and the default color scheme matched the core theme of the team responsible.

With the team’s Content Creation Coordinator helping me in providing me with content and helping me out with the placement of the content, the task of curating the template utilizing it’s customisable properties (like the well-explained grid system, the plugable left drawer, the readily available content templates, just to name a few). For accordion, text bubbles, tooltips, card elements I had to utilize the views of Semantic UI.

The responsive abilities of the CSS framework utilized was quite wholesome. Had to add additional responsive breakpoints and provide hide/show/collapse classes for all the breakpoints.

To develop the Registration Form of the Winter School [currently offline and replaced by Google Forms], to be organised by UBA, IIT Roorkee I used the native elements of the template. For the client-side form validation, I have used jquery Alphanum and Validate JS for a declarative way of validating things so that the code is maintainable for further development in future.
