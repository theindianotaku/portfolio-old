Description

Information Management Group (IMG), IIT Roorkee is a student group responsible for the development and maintenance of the main website of the institute and the intranet e-portal, Channel i.

Every year IMG recruits students from the fresher batch in their second semester. The recruitment process is a very crucial step in the development of any organisation. The aim of this website was; to explain the recruitment process and to make them understand who we are and our work values.

The website had to be professional in both, design and content. With the inspiration from the careers pages of various successful organisations like Whiteboard and Adobe, we designed the current page. Currently the page is offline so I have provided some screenshots of the project.
